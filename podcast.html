In this podcast, my discipleship and I attempted to answer important questions on how a biblical Worldview is important for everyday Christians. Some of the specific questions we tried to answer include;

  • Why is the order of the Bible important to the Bible Story?
  • How does the storyline of the Bible teach us about Jesus and his role in redemption?
  • How does "free will"come into play when thinking about Creation, Fall, and Redemption?
  • What are some ways you can look at both the Old and New Testaments as one cohesive book?
  • How should we read the Bible with a Biblical Worldview in mind?
  • Why is it important to look at worldy matters with a Biblical Worldview?
  • Can you remember a situation in your life where it was good that you approached it with a Biblical Worldview? Is there a time in your life where it would have been better to approach a situation with a Biblical Worldview?

Looking Back The Epistle books relate back to the rest of the Bible because the Apostles are writing to other churches throughout the world and teaching through writing how their actions need to be God-like and the dangers of not following God through faith in his son Jesus Christ. The letters are all about how the churches need to act like Jesus because he is the ultimate example of what a believer is
Looking Foward Looking forward to the last book of the Bible, Revelation. The Epistles are showing churches how to act and believe in God so that when the end comes, all who believe in God will meet in heaven
